PROJECTS

Flappy Bird AI

In this project, I explored the fascinating concept of Neuroevolution, applied to an age old game: Flappy Bird. A collection of Neural Networks, represented by a population of Flappy Birds, are trained by a Genetic Algorithm to detect and fly through a series of pipes. Put simply, these Flappy Birds are learning to make decisions…

TSP Genetic Algorithm

In this project, I’ve implemented a simple algorithm to suggest solutions to a modified version of a world renowned problem: the Travelling Salesman Problem. Having a worse case brute force complexity proportional to O(n!), a solution to this NP-Hard problem had to be autonomously intelligent. A genetic algorithm, due to its unqiue makeup, proved to be an apt solution…

OCR - Neural Networks

It was in this project where I first delved into the field of deep learning. Coded entirely in Java, this basic OCR (optical character recognition) application integrates a basic Neural Network with the back propagation training algorithm to create a unified learning program…

Intelligent Tic Tac Toe - Minimax

Sometimes described as the “staple” AI algorithm, Minimax is intriguing in its endless applications. In this project, I explore this relatively basic yet fascinating project to craft an unbeatable game of Tic Tac Toe. Try your luck against the algorithm by pressing here!

Logistic Regression - Theoretical Machine Learning
